Imagine you have a virtual desk where you can stick all your todo tasks and orgnize your time, in addition to this you can interact with your friends in order to do all your homeworks.

Shukudai allows you to do all this in one place.

Manage your tasks

You can add a new task with its importance or emergency and the system will show them to you and remind you everytime.

Manage your reminders

Shukudai allows you to choose an importante date in your own calendar and then remind you when it comes.

Manage your homeworks

Shukudai allows you to organize your homeworks by indexing them and much more things.

Talk to your friends

Shukudai-forum allows you to interact with other students or friends to share solutions and ideas.
